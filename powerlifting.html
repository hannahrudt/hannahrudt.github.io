I am visualizing the powerlifting dataset because I am personally interested in female athletes and the dataset provides many opportunities to break down the data into categories (e.g., by age, by event, by biological sex). The question I will answer with my visualization is: Have female athletes age 24-34 improved their squat and benchpress performance over time?
The plotted points have mouseover so when the pointer hovers, a text box appears on the top left of the graph with information about the athlete, date of event, and weight lifted (bench or squat). The legend can be clicked on to highlight each event.
One design choice I would have liked to incorporate was maintaining the bold text and increased stroke around the circles in the legend when clicked, but I didn't get a chance to figure it out.
